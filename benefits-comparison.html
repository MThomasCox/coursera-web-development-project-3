HSA vs HRA vs FSA - Differences Explained

Health Savings Account

A tax-advantaged account that lets you save and pay for qualified medical expenses if you have a high-deductible health plan.

Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA):

A pre-tax account funded by your employer or paycheck that pays for eligible medical, dental, and dependent care expenses, usually within the plan year.

Health Reimbursement Arrangements (HRAs):

An employer-funded account that reimburses employees for qualified medical expenses and, in some cases, insurance premiums.
